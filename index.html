The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born,
These "day names"have a deeper meaning regarding their their soul character.The naming ceremony by the Akan is shared through WestAfrica and African Diaspora.
Most Ghananian have at least one name from this system,even if they have English names or Christian names.
'Kwa' or 'Ko' are used for males whereas females 'aa'or 'wa' for women,these are affixes.
With this one will be able to know their correct Akan
name and its a guarantee that the results are highly correct as per the traditional naming system.
